The Project

Welcome to ESLAVO-SLAVA Festival, a breathtaking celebration of Slavic music that will transport you to the heart and soul of the vibrant Slavic music tradition! Join us from 7 to 9 august in the heart of Delta del Llobregat, as we unite music lovers in a harmonious exploration of the diverse sounds and rhythms that define Slavic musical heritage. Immerse yourself in a captivating fusion of traditional melodies and contemporary interpretations, brought to life by talented musicians and artists from across the Slavic diaspora. Experience the powerful emotions conveyed through haunting vocal performances, intricate instrumental arrangements, and dynamic rhythms that will stir your senses.

Indulge in the flavors of Slavic cuisine, marvel at the craftsmanship of traditional arts and crafts, and connect with a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for Slavic music. Whether you have a deep connection to your Slavic roots or are simply drawn to the allure of its distinctive sounds, ESLAVO-SLAVA promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you inspired and enriched. Mark your calendar and join us for a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Slavic music at ESLAVO-SLAVA!
